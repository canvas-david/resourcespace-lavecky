Content of testimony: Self-defense against antisemitic riots in Poland until the war against the Nazi army. 
In a group of self-defense against antisemitic riots in Boryslaw, killing of the commander of ZWIAZEK STRELECKI during confrontation with antisemitic group in 1935; prisoner in SWIETOKRZYSKIE prison; between the years 1935-1939 abuse of prisoners after the outbreak of war and liberation of prisoners by the Soviet army in KRASNYSTAW on 17.09.1939; service in the Red Army between the years 1940-1943; participation in battles.
