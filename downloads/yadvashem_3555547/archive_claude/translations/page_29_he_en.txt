about 20 km from Zhitomir. After my recovery I was referred to military unit 2146 and together with it

I participated in the defense of Moscow. At the end of the operation near Moscow they moved us in vehicles

to the Stalingrad area. During the military operations near Stalingrad I was wounded in the abdomen and because of this

I fell into northern captivity. I succeeded together with my comrades to break out from the north and cross to my unit. As a serious casualty

they transferred me to the area. They transferred me to a military hospital in the town BEREZNIKI in Ural

(Molotov district). In the hospital I lay for about 3 months. As a result of complications they removed my

spleen through surgery. From the hospital I was discharged as a war invalid in 1943. I went to DZAMBUL

in Kazakhstan. I received there a position as a technical supervisor of large garages for transport vehicles

that supplied the army. I received this position as a professional and war invalid. I used
